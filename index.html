// FAQ

Questions people usually ask

How long does a website take?

Timelines depend on scope, but smaller brochure sites move faster than database or ecommerce builds. The quote will include a realistic delivery range for your project.

Do you handle domain and hosting?

Yes. If you want, domain setup, hosting and the technical launch process can all be handled for you.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes. You can come with an old site, a half-finished idea, or a completely new project and we can rebuild from there.

Can you build private systems too?

Yes. Selected projects already include private school and farm operations tools, and custom internal features can be scoped where needed.
