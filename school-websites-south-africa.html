// School websites

School website design in South Africa.

Rapha Lumina designs school websites in South Africa for schools that need clearer communication, stronger public trust and easier access to admissions, updates and school information. Where needed, the work can also support private systems alongside the public website.

// What schools usually need

A school website should make information easier to find.

Admissions clarity

Parents should be able to find admissions steps, requirements, contact details and key school information without confusion.

School updates

News, notices, digest pages, galleries and event updates help the website stay useful instead of becoming a static placeholder.

Trust and presentation

A clear school website helps families feel more confident in the school, especially when the structure, tone and visual identity feel professional.

// Practical scope

Public website first, with room to support internal systems.

Some schools only need a public-facing website. Others need a site plus supporting digital tools for internal workflows. Rapha Lumina can work at either level depending on what the school actually needs.

  • ✓Admissions and contact pages
  • ✓School digest, notices or update sections
  • ✓Gallery and school information pages
  • ✓Private system support where appropriate
